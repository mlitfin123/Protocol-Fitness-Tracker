Privacy Policy

How Protocol collects, uses, and protects your information.

Last updated: April 9, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our fitness tracking application ("App").

By using the App, you agree to this policy.

2. Information We Collect

A. Account Information

  • Email address
  • Authentication credentials (handled securely via Supabase or auth provider)

B. Fitness & Usage Data

  • Workout logs
  • Nutrition entries (calories, macros)
  • Body weight and progress data
  • Check-ins and notes

C. Device & Technical Data

  • Device type
  • App usage data (basic analytics, if enabled)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to provide core app functionality, track your fitness progress, improve user experience, identify app performance issues, and enable optional future features such as coaching services.

We do not sell your personal data.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties except service providers such as Supabase infrastructure, or legal requirements if required by law.

Your fitness data is private and tied to your account.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Data is stored securely using industry-standard practices.
  • Authentication is handled via secure providers.
  • We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active.

If you delete your account, all personal and fitness data will be permanently deleted.

7. Your Rights

You can access your data within the app and delete your account at any time.

For additional requests: Contact mlitfin@elevarefit.org

8. Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for users under 13.

We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy.

Updates will be reflected with a new "Effective Date."
